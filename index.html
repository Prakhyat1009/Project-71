The dreams of Seth Anandram Jaipuria took concrete shape and became a reality when the foundation was laid by our Late Prime Minister Smt. Indira Gandhi on 13th September, 1966. The school became fully operational after the inauguration done by our Late President Dr. Fakhruddin Ali Ahmad on 26th October, 1974.

school library

Was built in 1966

Library, traditionally, collection of books used for reading or study, or the building or room in which such a collection is kept. The word derives from the Latin liber, “book,” whereas a Latinized Greek word, bibliotheca, is the origin of the word for library in German, Russian, and the Romance languages.

school assembly hall

Was built in 1966

It is a necessary and centrally located unit of a school plant. In most schools, the assembly hall has multiple uses including an auditorium where recreational activities and celebrations could take place, daily prayers can be held.

school chemistry lab

Was built in 1966

The purpose of the laboratory component of the course is several-fold. It reinforces the material you have learned in class and it gives you a chance to apply your knowledge. You will learn some important experimental techniques that are necessary for you to become an effective chemist.

school computer room

Was built in 1966

The basic function of the Computer Room is to improve teaching-educational and scientific process through interactive teaching and exercises, by releasing presentations over video-projectors. The Computer Room is equipped with 26 computers, video-projector and a permanent internet connection.

school achievements

Education World Elite Ranking no.1

Education World Elite Ranking no.1

The status of Seth Anandram Jaipuria School, Kanpur goes beyond the City and the State Level to scale up to the Education World Elite Ranking India No. 1. The event took place in Delhi on 29th September, 2018.This achievement was based on a step wise Online Evaluation of the Principal, then the Teachers and Students. It was conducted by a panel of Jury connected with Ivy League University and Edfinity, USA along with Education World Ranking Team.

ISA Awards

ISA Awards

It was a moment of pride and honor when Seth Anandram Jaipuria School, Kanpur was recognized for its International endeavours in the field of education by the ISA award for the second consecutive term – 2018-2021. The award was presented in a felicitation ceremony held on Dec. 5, 2018 at New Delhi.The ISA is a global accreditation scheme which acknowledges good practices in incorporating the International dimension into schools.

The School Excellence Award by Brain Feed

The School Excellence Award by Brain Feed

It was yet another glorious moment for our school when it bagged ‘The School Excellence Award’ by Brain Feed for the year 2017-18. Mr. M.K. Mishra, the Senior Academic Head, received the award on behalf of the School. The ceremony was held at Lucknow on 20th January, 2018.

News

  • “Sadbhavana” Inter-School Cultural Fest on the World Nature Conservation Day – 2021

    Our School participated in Sadbhavana – an event organized by Seth Anandram Jaipuria School, Vasundhara, Ghaziabad on 28th July,2021- the World Nature Conservation Day.The event aimed towards the sustainable development and encouraged the students to participate in protection, conservation and sustainable management of natural resources. Almost fifty schools participated in this Inter School Fest. It was a proud moment for the Mighty Jaipurians when our students bagged three prizes out of five events.

  • First virtual COMFEST 2020

    COMFEST, a unique celebration of technological advancements, is a rejuvenating ride for students and a legacy that began in 2001. The event has evolved as the biggest student-organized festival in the nation, hosting national and international schools. This year, the school is presenting the twentieth edition of this mega-event by hosting 700 students from more than 40 schools connected virtually.
